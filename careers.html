Let's schedule an interview

One of our recruiters will get in contact with you to discuss the position, briefly talk about your motivation for the job and possibly schedule a first interview.

Let's get to know each other

In the first interview you will meet the Hiring Manager, Scrum Master or a Team Member. Together with the Recruiter you will do a deep dive into your working experience. This interview also gives you the possibility to get an image of the vacant position.

Let's see what you can do

When invited for the second interview we send out two assessments; a personality questionnaire and a capacity test. You can make these from the comfort of you home and they will take about 50 minutes. When needed the Recruiter might also send you a position specific assignment.

Review and assessment

The Recruiter will again include the Hiring Manager, Scrum Master or a Team Member in this interview. When somebody new is included we will do a brief introduction round and in the end of the interview we will discuss the outcome of the assessments and assignment.

Offer

The Recruiter will send the job offer to you by mail and will give you a call afterward to walk you through. When you accept the offer our HR department will make sure you receive the contract via mail which you can sign online.
